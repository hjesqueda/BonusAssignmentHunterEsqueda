Cleaning Methods

Explore various innovative techniques used to clean plastic from the ocean:

Ocean Booms

Large floating barriers designed to trap plastic debris on the ocean's surface, preventing it from spreading further.

Cleanup Robots

Autonomous devices that can navigate and collect trash from the ocean and coastlines efficiently.

Community Drives

Local volunteer efforts to gather plastic waste from beaches, promoting awareness and engagement.
